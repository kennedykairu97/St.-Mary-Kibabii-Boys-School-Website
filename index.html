THE KIBABII BOYS HIGH

Welcome to Kibabii Boys High School. People from all walks of life and all parts of the world have been visiting us for 20 years and we are delighted that via this website you are joining that long tradition. Our aim is to remain at the forefront of centers of learning, teaching and research

WHY CHOOSE KIBABII HIGH

 Over the past years we have produced students who have gone to make great and positive contributions in our society. We have perfected the art of training our students to become top achievers in all areas of life. We have a well trained staff that will ensure all our students get the best education and achieve their full potential. We not only strive for academic excellence but it is also our goal to ensure that all our students develop both intellectually, physically, mentally, emotionally, socially and spiritually.

 With our core values of Faith, Integrity, Non Discrimination, Quality and Service, it is our aim to be a premier institution in producing graduates who will serve humanity with distinction. So bring your son to our school and we promise to train him on values and nurture him intellectually and prepare him to positively contribute to our country and planet.
